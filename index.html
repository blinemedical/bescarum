1. Define Probability & Statistics

Probability

  • What is Probability (P)? A measure of the the likelihood an event will occur.
  • \(p=0\) - the event will not occur
  • \(p=1\) - the event will occur
  • Bayesian: probability is deduced from prior knowledge
  • Frequentist: probability obtained from experimentation

Statistics

  • What is Statistic? Information that can be computed from data

Schools of Thought
